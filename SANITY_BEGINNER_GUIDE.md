# 🌟 DancerGirl Sanity - Super Simple Guide for Beginners

## 🚀 QUICK START - Most Common Tasks

### Want to write a new story?
1. Click **"🚀 QUICK START"** at the top
2. Click **"📝 Write a New Story/Article"**
3. Fill in the form
4. Done! ✨

### Want to add a new dancer?
1. Click **"🚀 QUICK START"** at the top
2. Click **"💃 Add a Dancer Profile"**
3. Fill in their info
4. Done! ✨

## 📁 WHERE TO FIND THINGS

### 📰 STORIES & ARTICLES
This is where ALL your written content lives:
- **➕ Write New Story** = Create a new article
- **📚 View All Stories** = See everything you've written
- **⭐ Featured Stories Only** = Stories that appear on homepage

### 👥 PEOPLE & PROFILES
All the amazing people in your community:
- **💃 Dancers** = Dance artists
- **✨ Choreographers** = Dance creators
- **🌟 Dancer of the Month** = Special monthly features

### 📅 EVENTS
Everything happening in the dance world:
- **➕ Create New Event** = Add a workshop, show, or class
- **🎉 View All Events** = See all upcoming events

## 💡 PRO TIPS FOR BEGINNERS

### Making a story appear on the homepage:
1. When writing/editing a story
2. Look for the **"Feature on Homepage? 🌟"** checkbox
3. Turn it ON ✅
4. Save!

### Can't find something?
- Check **"❓ NEED HELP?"** at the bottom
- Look in **"🌟 Recently Updated"** to find your recent work

### Organizing stories by topic:
- Use **Categories** (like "Money Moves", "Dancer Speak Up")
- Find them in **⚙️ SETTINGS → 🏷️ Categories**

---

## 🎯 SIMPLE WORKFLOW

### To publish a new story:
1. **QUICK START** → **Write New Story**
2. Add:
   - Title (your headline)
   - Excerpt (short preview)
   - Main Image (cover photo)
   - Body (your full story)
3. Check **"Featured"** if you want it on homepage
4. Click **"Publish"**

That's it! You're done! 🎉

---

## ❓ CONFUSED?

Remember:
- **Stories** = Articles, blog posts, written content
- **Featured** = Shows on homepage
- **Categories** = Topics like "Money Moves"
- **Excerpt** = Short preview text

Just start with **QUICK START** and you'll be fine! 💪
