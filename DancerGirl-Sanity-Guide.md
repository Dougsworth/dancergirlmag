## Stories & Articles

This is the heart of DancerGirl. Every article on the site lives here.

### Creating a New Article

1. Click **Stories & Articles** in the sidebar
2. Click **All Articles**
3. Click the **+** button (top right) to create a new article
4. Fill in the fields:

**The important fields:**

- **Article Headline** -- Make it catchy. This is what people see first.
- **Website Link** -- Click "Generate" after writing your headline. This creates the URL.
- **Article Preview** -- A short teaser (2-3 sentences) that appears on the homepage and article lists. Make people want to click.
- **Cover Photo** -- Upload a high-quality image. This is the main photo people see when browsing.
- **Which Section?** -- This is how the article gets sorted on the site:
  - **Money Moves** -- Business tips, career advice, financial guidance for dancers
  - **Choreographers Corner** -- Spotlights on choreographers and their work
  - **Dancers: Speak Up** -- Dancer voices, interviews, personal stories
  - **General** -- Everything else (culture, history, festivals, etc.)
- **Write Your Article** -- This is the full article. You can format text (bold, italic, headings), add images, embed YouTube videos, and create lists.
- **Who Wrote This?** -- Enter the author's name.

**Optional but useful:**

- **Feature on Homepage?** -- Turn this ON to highlight the article on the homepage.
- **Mark as Trending?** -- Adds a "Trending" badge for hot content.
- **When to Publish?** -- Defaults to right now. You can schedule articles for the future.

### Finding Articles by Section

Instead of scrolling through all articles, use the shortcuts:

- Click **Stories & Articles** > **Money Moves** to see only Money Moves articles
- Click **Stories & Articles** > **Choreographers Corner** for choreographer content
- Click **Stories & Articles** > **Dancers: Speak Up** for dancer voice pieces
- Click **Stories & Articles** > **Featured Articles** to see what's currently featured on the homepage

## Dancer of the Month

This is where you celebrate the dancers who define Caribbean dance culture.

### Adding a New Dancer of the Month

1. Click **Dancer of the Month** in the sidebar
2. Click **Archive** (this shows all entries)
3. Click the **+** button to create a new feature
4. Fill in:

**Basic Information:**
- **Dancer's Name** -- Their full name as you want it displayed
- **Which Month?** -- Select the month
- **What Year?** -- Enter the year
- **Website Link** -- Click "Generate" after filling in the name, month, and year
- **Social Media Links** -- Add their Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, etc.

**Story & Content:**
- **Tell Their Story** -- Write the full feature article about this dancer. Their background, journey, what makes them special.
- **Short Preview Text** -- A brief hook (under 300 characters) that appears in the listing. Make it exciting.
- **What Dance Styles?** -- Tag their styles (Dancehall, Soca, Afrobeats, etc.)

**Photos & Videos:**
- **Main Cover Photo** -- The big hero image for their feature page. Make it stunning.
- **Photo Gallery** -- Additional photos (performance shots, behind-the-scenes, etc.)
- **Video Highlights** -- Add YouTube or TikTok links showcasing their best work.

**Awards & Achievements:**
- Add competitions won, performances, milestones -- anything that shows their talent.

**Display & Settings:**
- **Make This the Main Featured Dancer?** -- Turn ON for the current month's dancer. This puts them front and center on the site. **Only one dancer should have this on at a time.** When you feature a new dancer, turn it OFF on the previous one.
- **Show on Website?** -- Turn OFF to hide a draft or temporarily remove an entry.

### Managing Current vs Archive

- **Dancer of the Month** > **Current Feature** -- Shows whoever has the "Featured" toggle ON
- **Dancer of the Month** > **Archive** -- Shows all dancers ever featured

When a new month comes:
1. Open the current featured dancer, turn OFF "Make This the Main Featured Dancer?"
2. Create the new dancer entry, turn ON "Make This the Main Featured Dancer?"

That's it -- the old one automatically moves to the archive.

## Letters from the Editor

This is your personal space, Shanice. Your letters to the DancerGirl community.

### Writing a New Letter

1. Click **Letters from the Editor** in the sidebar
2. Click the **+** button
3. Fill in:

- **Letter Subject** -- Like an email subject line. What's this letter about?
- **Website Link** -- Click "Generate" after writing the subject.
- **Cover Photo** -- Optional but makes the letter look more inviting in listings.
- **Letter Preview** -- A short preview that appears on the homepage and in the letter list.
- **Write Your Letter** -- Pour your heart out. Share your thoughts on dance, culture, the community. You can add photos here too.
- **Who's Writing?** -- Select yourself (Founder profile).
- **When to Send?** -- Schedule it or publish immediately.
- **Feature This Letter?** -- Turn ON to make it prominent on the site.
- **Letter Topics** -- Add tags like "culture", "community", "carnival" to help readers find related letters.

## Events

Post dance events, workshops, festivals, and classes happening across the Caribbean.

### Adding a New Event

1. Click **Events** in the sidebar
2. Click the **+** button
3. Fill in:

**Event Basics:**
- **Event Name** -- Make it descriptive and exciting
- **Website Link** -- Click "Generate"
- **Event Details** -- Full description. What will happen? Who should come? What to expect?

**When & Where:**
- **When Does It Start?** -- Date and time
- **When Does It End?** -- Optional
- **Does This Repeat?** -- For recurring events like weekly classes
- **Where Is It Happening?** -- Choose between in-person (venue, address, city, country) or online (paste the meeting link)

**Registration & Tickets:**
- **How Much Does It Cost?** -- Enter 0 for free events
- **Do People Need to Sign Up?** -- If yes, paste the registration/ticket link

**Photos & Tags:**
- **Event Photo** -- A flyer or photo from a previous edition
- **Event Tags** -- Keywords like "beginner-friendly", "carnival", "workshop"

## Music & Media

### Videos

For standalone videos (music videos, dance clips, interviews).

1. Click **Music & Media** > **Videos**
2. Click **+** to add a new video
3. Fill in the title, paste the YouTube/Vimeo URL, upload a thumbnail, and categorize it.

### Playlists

For curated music collections -- Spotify, Apple Music, or YouTube playlists.

1. Click **Music & Media** > **Playlists**
2. Click **+** to add a new playlist
3. Fill in:
   - **Title** -- Name of the playlist
   - **Platform** -- Spotify, Apple Music, or YouTube
   - **Playlist URL** -- Paste the public link
   - **Description** -- What vibe is this playlist?
   - **Tags** -- Keywords like "soca", "dancehall", "workout"

## Tips for Great Content

**Photos matter.** A stunning cover photo gets 3x more clicks than a plain one. Use high-quality images -- performance shots, candid moments, vibrant colors that reflect Caribbean energy.

**Headlines sell the story.** Instead of "Interview with Dancer", try "How This Kingston Dancer Is Rewriting the Rules of Dancehall". Make people curious.

**Previews are your hook.** The article preview appears everywhere -- homepage, social shares, search results. Write it like a movie trailer. Tease the best part.

**Schedule content.** You don't have to publish everything right away. Write a few articles, schedule them across the week. Consistent content keeps people coming back.

**Use the Featured toggle wisely.** Only feature your best content on the homepage. If everything is featured, nothing stands out.
